About the company

EigenMethod is a young company, we have more than 50 high-qualified developers. Our main activity is connected with the development of our own software products. Also, we help other companies to successfully realize their projects on creating software.

We use cutting-edge informational technologies and methodologies of development for working out our own products. Creating of software products is realized with the help of the technology stack: Python, C++, PostgreSQL, Django, Twisted, Tornado, Redis and others.

We have the experience of developing software when tight integration with SAP systems is required. We provide services of outsourcing developments on SAP HANA, SAPUI5 и ABAP.

Our offices are in the Eastern Europe where many IT-talentscan be found. Our team is not only highly competent, but also cost-effective both for our clients and for developing our own products.
