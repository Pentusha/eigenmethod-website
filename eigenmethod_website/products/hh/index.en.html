What is HyperHive?

HyperHive is an innovational product at the market of Mobile Application Development Platform solutions (MADP), allowing developers and companies to quickly develop and implement mobile solutions because of availability of large number of ready templates for solving typical tasks collected into a unified platform

Mobile platform HyperHive is a “turnkey basis” technology stack of ready scenarios and templates for performing typical tasks of mobile development.

Integration of the platform in the architecture of the solution is carried out in two simple steps:

Connecting different data sources in the user-friendly interface with the minimum number of steps and parameters.

Connecting HyperHive framework into the mobile application project.
